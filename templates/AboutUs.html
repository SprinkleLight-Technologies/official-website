About Us

At Sprinklelight Technologies, we're passionate about harnessing the power of technology to create innovative solutions that inspire and empower. Our name reflects our commitment to innovation and our goal to brighten the digital landscape.

We specialize in crafting stunning websites and developing robust mobile applications for both Android and iOS platforms. Our team of skilled professionals is dedicated to delivering exceptional digital experiences that exceed expectations.

Our journey began in January 2021 with "TechforIndia", an Instagram tech page that quickly gained traction, reaching over 20.5K followers. We leveraged this platform to share valuable insights and even collaborated with renowned mobile brands like iQOO through targeted advertising campaigns.

In 2023, we expanded our services to include app and web development, marking a significant milestone in our growth. To align with this evolution, we rebranded "TechforIndia" as Sprinklelight Technologies and transitioned our tech page to its current name, tech.brunch.

To keep you informed about the latest tech trends and industry insights, we invite you to explore our tech page, tech.brunch. Here, we share curated news articles, tutorials, and expert opinions on a wide range of tech topics.
